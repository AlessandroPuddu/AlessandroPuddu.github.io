About Me

I am a passionate and dedicated Unity Developer, with a degree in Computer Science from Politecnico di Torino. I have developed a strong foundation in programming and software development, with a particular focus on game development in Unity3D. My experience includes working on challenging projects, such as 'Alaloth - Champions of The Four Kingdoms', an action RPG game, and 'Hell For One', a blend of action and strategy elements, where I used my proficient knowledge of C# and Unity extensively.

With a strong grasp of Object-Oriented Programming and game design principles, I have been able to create immersive and engaging game experiences. I am known for my quick learning abilities and commitment to keeping up with the latest advancements in technology. My ultimate goal is to create games that players love and remember.
