Alessandro Puddu - Unity Developer Portfolio

About Me

I am a dedicated Unity Developer with a breadth of skills spanning gameplay engineering, AI development, shader programming, and more. With a rich background in Computer Science from the University of Milan, I have used my skills to contribute to a variety of immersive games, including the RPG 'Alaloth: Champions of The Four Kingdoms' and 'Apocalypse Rangers', a post-apocalyptic online twin-stick shooter.

My technical prowess extends beyond gameplay, with demonstrated proficiency in AR/VR application development, networking, and an array of development tools like Unity, C#, Git, Asana, Jira, Visual Studio, and JetBrains Rider. A committed learner, I am also actively enhancing my skills in C++ and Unreal Engine. Beyond my technical abilities, I bring a keen understanding of SOLID principles, design patterns, Agile development methodology, and a strong team spirit.

When not developing games, I can be found exploring various game genres as an avid gamer, and playing guitar and bass guitar as an amateur musician, expressing my creativity beyond the digital realm.

RuNeglect

Date: 2018-2019

For my Bachelor's thesis at the AIS-Lab, University of Milan, I developed "RuNeglect," a 2.5D endless runner game on Microsoft HoloLens intended for rehabilitating patients affected by hemispatial neglect. It utilized HoloLens spatial recognition capabilities to allow players to choose a surface for the game area, providing an immersive and therapeutic experience.
